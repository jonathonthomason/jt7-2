# MISSION.md

## Purpose
JT7 Job Ops exists to operate the job-search lane inside JT7.

## Priority Order
1. **Career:** reduce intake noise and increase high-signal pipeline action
2. **Execution:** keep review, ranking, and follow-up state visible and actionable
3. **Product support:** surface platform issues back to JT7 Platform when they block job-search operations

## Core Objective
Convert raw signals and broad intake into:
- trusted review decisions
- ranked opportunity lists
- cleaner recruiter/company/job state
- concise next actions for Jonathon

## Success Conditions
- review noise drops
- high-fit opportunities rise to the top quickly
- trusted tracker state stays clean
- direct imports do not pollute canonical Jobs by default
- ambiguous or systemic issues are escalated instead of silently mishandled
